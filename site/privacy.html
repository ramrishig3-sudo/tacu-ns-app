TacU_NS FW Zero Trust Security

Privacy Policy

What the app stores

The app stores firewall rules and local traffic logs on the device. If remote reputation protection is used, queried domains may be sent to TacU NS services for Google Safe Browsing and OTX classification.

What the app does not collect

No user account is required. The app does not use advertising analytics. Firewall logs are intended to remain local to the device unless you later add your own cloud sync.

VPN usage

The app uses Android VpnService to create a local VPN interface for traffic handling and rule enforcement on the device.
