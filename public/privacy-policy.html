Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at TacU- NS: Network Security Pro ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Because we value your privacy, our core operational principle is data minimization. We do not collect, store, or farm your personal IP address, browsing history, or physical location data.

2. How We Handle Network Scans

When you use the Privacy Shield or Threat Intelligence modules, the data required to perform network analysis is processed temporarily. Queries to external intelligence communities (like global security vendors) are heavily anonymized. We do not transmit your personal identifiable information (PII) to any third-party risk analysis engines.

3. Device Permissions

To operate effectively as a network analyzer, we may request certain device permissions (such as local Network State access). This data never leaves your device maliciously and is solely used to populate your local application dashboard with diagnostic information.

4. Use of Third-Party APIs

Our application interfaces with various community threat databases and Neural Engine models. These are strictly used as diagnostic tools to return threat severity scores. We expressly do not aggregate or sell your scan queries.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy notice from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Revised" date and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this notice, you may email us at our developer contact address listed on the Google Play Store.
