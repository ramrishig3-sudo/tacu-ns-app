# Privacy Policy

TacU_NS FW Zero Trust Security is an Android firewall application with local enforcement and optional remote domain reputation checks.

- The app stores firewall rules and traffic logs on the device.
- The app can send queried domains to TacU NS services for Google Safe Browsing and AlienVault OTX reputation checks.
- The app does not require user registration.
- The app does not send analytics, telemetry, or security events to a remote server in v1.
- The app uses Android `VpnService` to observe and manage traffic metadata for local firewall decisions.
- Users can clear locally stored logs from inside the app.

Support contact: `support@tacuns.net`
